## 1. Summary

- Most information you enter is stored **locally on your device** in an on-device database (**Hive**). We do **not** operate our own cloud account or central user database for routine app use.
- The App may show **Google AdMob** banner advertisements. AdMob and its partners may process **device and advertising-related data** as described in **Section 5** and in [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Backup and transfer** features let you export, import, or move data between your devices. You may protect exports with a **password** (encryption on device before sharing). We do **not** receive copies of those transfers.
- The **current release** does **not** include an in-app AI chat connected to external servers. Optional cloud-sync UI in code is **not** offered in the settings screen you see today.
- The App is **not** a medical device and does **not** replace professional care (see **Section 10**).
- We do **not sell** your personal information.

This summary is for convenience only. The full policy below controls.

## 2. Information stored on your device

The App helps you track pregnancy and postpartum-related information. Depending on how you use it, **locally stored** data may include, for example:

- Profile and pregnancy context (e.g. display name you set, due date / week tracking, milestone flags)
- Health diary, symptoms, medications and intake logs, important tests, medical record references and attachments you add
- Reminders and notification schedules you configure
- Journal entries and images you attach (e.g. belly photos)
- Baby-related logs (growth, feeding, sleep, hygiene, vaccination records you enter)
- Expense records you enter
- App preferences (e.g. **language**, ad display caps, backup-related settings)

**Sensitive data:** Much of this is **health- or pregnancy-related** and may be sensitive. It stays on your device unless you export, share, back up, or transfer it using features **you** start, or unless your **operating system** backs up app data (e.g. device backup to iCloud or Google).

**Deletion:** Use in-app delete or reset options where available. Uninstalling the App may remove local data depending on your platform and backup settings.

## 4. Backup, export, import, and device transfer

When you use **Data management** (or related flows), you may:

- **Export** app data to a file on your device (optionally **encrypted with a password** you choose before sharing the file)
- **Import** a backup file (including password-protected files you created)
- **Send** or **receive** a backup to/from another phone on your local network (e.g. QR code + transfer URL)

**Important:**

- Backup files can contain **all** categories of data listed in Section 2. Treat exported files like sensitive medical/personal records.
- Password-protected exports are encrypted **on your device** before you save or share them. If you lose the password, we **cannot** recover the file for you.
- **Share**, **email**, **cloud drives**, and **device-to-device transfer** use channels **you** select; their privacy practices apply to the destination you choose.
- We do **not** automatically upload your health content to our servers through these features.

## 5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses **Google AdMob** to show **banner ads** on some screens.

When ads are shown, **Google and its partners** may collect and process information such as:

- Device identifiers used for advertising (where allowed by the platform and your settings)
- IP address, coarse location derived from IP, device type, operating system, app version
- Ad interaction data (e.g. impressions, clicks) for advertising and fraud prevention

Processing is governed by **Google’s policies**, including:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

**iOS App Tracking Transparency:** On iPhone and iPad, the App may show Apple’s system prompt asking whether to allow tracking across other companies’ apps and websites. This uses the advertising identifier (IDFA) only if you tap **Allow**. If you tap **Ask App Not to Track**, ads may still appear but are typically less personalized. You can change your choice later in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**.

You can limit ad personalization on your device:

- **Android:** Google Settings → Ads, or equivalent device settings.  
- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising (options vary by iOS version).

The App may apply **content rating** and **frequency limits** for ads in the App’s code; this does not change that Google’s SDK processes data as described above when ads load.

## 6. Email, links, and other third parties

**Feedback:** If you use menu actions that open your **email client**, you send the message yourself; we only receive what you choose to include.

**Share / export:** Content leaves your device through the **system share sheet** or app you pick.

**Educational links and videos:** The App may open external sites (e.g. YouTube) in your browser or an in-app view. Those services have their own privacy policies.

**AI assistant (not in current release):** The codebase includes optional integration with **Google’s Generative AI (Gemini)** for a chat feature, but it is **turned off** in the shipping configuration (`AIConfig.isEnabled = false`) and is **not** linked from the App menu in this release. If we enable it in a future version, we will update this policy to describe that messages (and optional images you send) are processed by **Google** according to [Google’s privacy terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy), and that chat history may be stored locally on your device.

**Cloud sync:** There is experimental/placeholder code for cloud providers, but the **cloud sync controls are not shown** in the current Data management UI. We do **not** sync your data to Google Drive, iCloud, Dropbox, or similar on your behalf in this release.
