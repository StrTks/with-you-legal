## 1. Summary

- Most information you enter in the App is stored **locally on your device** (on-device database). We do **not** operate our own cloud account or central user database for routine app use.
- The App may show **Google AdMob** advertisements. AdMob and its partners may process **device and advertising-related data** as described in **Section 4** and in [Google’s Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- The App may use **camera**, **photo library**, **notifications**, and **local network** features only when needed for the features you choose. Details are in **Section 3**.
- We do **not sell** your personal information.

This summary is for convenience only. The full policy below controls.

## 2. Information stored on your device

The App is designed to help you track pregnancy and postpartum-related information. Depending on how you use the App, **locally stored** data may include, for example:

- Pregnancy profile and related notes (e.g. due date context, tracker entries)
- Health diary entries, symptoms, medications and intake logs, important tests, medical file references you attach
- Reminders and local notifications you configure
- Journal entries and images you choose to attach
- Baby-related logs (e.g. growth, feeding, sleep, hygiene, vaccination records you enter)
- Expense records you enter
- App preferences such as **language** and **local settings** related to features (e.g. ad display caps stored only on device)

This data remains on your device unless you use **export, backup, share, or device transfer** features you explicitly start, or unless your **operating system** backs up app data according to your device settings.

**Deletion:** You can remove much of this data from within the App where the UI provides delete or reset options. Uninstalling the App may remove local data depending on your platform; follow your device manufacturer’s guidance if you need complete removal.

## 4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses **Google AdMob** to show **banner ads** in some screens.

When ads are shown, **Google and its partners** may collect and process information such as:

- Device identifiers used for advertising (where allowed by the platform and your settings)
- IP address, coarse location derived from IP, device type, operating system, app version
- Ad interaction data (e.g. impressions, clicks) for advertising and fraud prevention

Processing is governed by **Google’s policies**, including:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

You can limit ad personalization on your device:

- **Android:** Google Settings → Ads, or equivalent device settings.  
- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising / Tracking (options vary by iOS version).

The App may apply **content rating** and **frequency limits** for ads in the App’s code; this does not change that Google’s SDK processes data as described above when ads load.
